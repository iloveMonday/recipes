King Soup

Description

This is a locally renowned soup. A soup of legend. A glistening example of grass-roots, word-of-mouth marketing.
Tucked about two thirds of the way down the right column on the back of Vientiane Cafe's menu is the King Soup: a coconut curry based entree with glass noodles and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • coconut milk
  • curry paste
  • vegetable broth
  • keffir lime leaves
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • onion
  • green pepper
  • fried tofu
  • a kiss

Steps

  1. saute the ginger, garlic, and onion
  2. add the curry paste and brother
  3. toss in the vegetables
  4. add that lil lime leaf and top it off with a kiss
